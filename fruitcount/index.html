Fruit Count:with the Fruitkids

Welcome to the orchards. Come play with the Fruitkids! The Fruitkids need you to help them play in their orchards and collect all of their fruit. Fruit Count is a fun and educational board game for iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch that helps learn the basics of counting along the way.

App Features

Fruit Count is the entertaining counting game that features:

  • Fun for all ages, especially young minds
  • Supports 1 to 4 players
  • Classic board game entertainment
  • To win, be the first player to pick your tree clean
  • Play against the computer, or pass-and-play with friends
  • Introduces early math concepts like counting, addition and subtraction
  • Improves fine motor skill as you play
  • Upbeat, fun music and sound effects
  • "Hand-held" or "Table-top" game options
